About Google Cloud Next

Next ‘17 will be the largest event in Google’s history. Google Cloud engineers, customers and partners will gather for inspiration and hands on experiences with tools, technologies and services born in the Cloud, including the latest developments from Google Cloud Platform, G Suite, Maps and Devices.

This year's Google Cloud Next 2017 Extended Cebu will be held on April 22, 2017 at A Space Cebu, Crossroads, Gov M Cuenco Avenue, Banilad, Cebu City.
